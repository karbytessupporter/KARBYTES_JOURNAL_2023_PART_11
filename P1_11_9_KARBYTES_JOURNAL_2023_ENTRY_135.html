My least favorite people are those who seem to focus on other people’s business rather than on their own. Such people seem to go out of their way to harass me for the “crime” of being self-focused instead of caught up in petty politics like they are. I see them doing little more than demanding to interrupt me and protest noisily when I am in the middle of doing something which absorbs my mental focus and which is not all about entertaining and flattering them. Few people seem to actually mind their own business nor value increasing their own intelligence. Those who do are those I generally admire, want to support, and want to emulate to some extent.
